# Strategy

Roadmaps, vision, and business context.

## Folders

| Folder | What's Here |
|--------|-------------|
| `roadmaps/` | Quarterly roadmaps, planning docs, milestone tracking |
| `vision/` | North-star vision, multi-year direction, principles |
| `business-context/` | Market sizing, JTBD, ICP, business model — slow-changing context |

## Conventions

- **Roadmaps** are dated (`q2-2026-roadmap.md`). Keep prior quarters; add new ones.
- **Vision** docs are usually long-lived. When a vision changes, log a decision (`decisions/`) and update the vision doc.
- **Business context** is the doc you point new hires at to understand the market and the business model. Update annually or on major shifts.

## When to Write Each

- **Quarterly planning** → new file in `roadmaps/`
- **Strategy shift** → log a `decision`, update `vision/` if direction changed, update `business-context/` if the market read changed
- **New JTBD insight** → update `business-context/`, link customer call summaries that prompted it
