# Processes

Operational processes and checklists. Stable, low-frequency-update reference docs.

## What Goes Here

- Customer onboarding checklists
- Feature launch run-books
- Incident response process
- Quarterly planning process
- Hiring loops

## What Doesn't Go Here

- **One-off decisions** → `decisions/`
- **Recurring multi-step workflows you run with Claude** → `workflows/`
- **Engineering-specific run-books** → `engineering/plans/{area}/`

Processes here are role-agnostic — anyone on the team should be able to follow them.
