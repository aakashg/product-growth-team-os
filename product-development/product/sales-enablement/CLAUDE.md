# Sales Enablement

Sales-facing docs, pitch decks, customer-onboarding flows, objection handling.

## What Goes Here

- Pitch deck source markdown (the canonical version — exports go to Google Slides)
- Customer onboarding playbooks
- Objection handling guides (with the latest competitive intel referenced)
- Sales talk tracks for new feature launches
- Buyer persona briefs

## What Doesn't Go Here

- **Customer call summaries** → `customers/accounts/{customer}/calls/summaries/`
- **Competitive intel** → `competitive-research/competitors/{competitor}/`
- **Pricing decisions** → `decisions/`
- **PRDs** → `PRDs/{area}/`

Sales enablement is the *output* layer that consumes from competitive research, customer insights, and decisions.
