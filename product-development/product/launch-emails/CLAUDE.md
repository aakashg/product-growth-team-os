# Launch Emails

Customer-facing launch communications.

## What Goes Here

- Launch announcement emails (the markdown source — exports go to your email tool)
- In-product release notes (long-form, suitable for a changelog page)
- Customer migration emails for breaking changes

## Naming

`{feature-slug}-launch.md` — keep one file per launch.

## What Doesn't Go Here

- **Sales talk tracks** → `sales-enablement/`
- **Internal launch announcements** → Slack, not the repo
- **Press releases** → marketing tool, not here unless you want them archived
