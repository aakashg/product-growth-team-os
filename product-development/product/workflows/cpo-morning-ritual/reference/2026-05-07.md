## How to Reproduce This Output

```sh
# Against your team's repo (with portfolio.yaml populated)
/portfolio-pulse --mode=daily
```

The skill reads `accounts/portfolio.yaml`, the last 14 days of summaries in `accounts/{slug}/calls/summaries/`, recent `decisions/`, and any connected MCPs (Calendar, Granola, Slack, CRM, LinkedIn). It produces the brief above in under 30 seconds. Drafts go to a review queue; nothing auto-sends.

For the workflow that wraps this with calendar integration and meeting briefs, see `workflows/cpo-morning-ritual/CLAUDE.md`.
