# Product Context

Reference docs for the systems and concepts inside your product. Slow-changing context that helps a new team member understand how the product actually works under the hood.

## What Goes Here

- Platform overview — the conceptual model of how your product works
- Feature glossary — what each major feature does and how it relates to others
- Object model — the core entities (project, account, generation, deploy, etc.) and their relationships
- Architecture diagrams (high-level, not engineering-specific)
- Glossary of internal terms

## What Doesn't Go Here

- **Engineering RFCs** → `engineering/rfcs/{area}/`
- **PRDs** → `PRDs/{area}/`
- **External-facing docs** → your public docs site, not this repo

This folder is the "if I'm a new PM, what do I need to read to understand how the product works" doc set.
